I am Er. Arun Panthi, a dedicated and detail-oriented Civil Engineer committed to building Nepal's future through quality engineering and innovative construction practices.

Graduating with a Bachelor of Engineering in Civil Engineering from Mid-West University, I have developed expertise in structural analysis, geotechnical engineering, construction management, and transportation engineering.

My professional experience includes supervising a multi-story hotel construction project where I managed RCC works, quality control, and team coordination. I also completed a rewarding 4-month internship at Road Division Surkhet (Department of Roads), gaining hands-on experience in government infrastructure projects, surveying, estimation, and road construction.
